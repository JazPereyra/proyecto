The different types of coffe

Here, we’ll introduce you to a few popular coffee varieties, each with its unique flavor and preparation method.

Espresso

Strong coffee brewed by forcing hot water
through finely-ground beans, creating a rich, concentrated shot.

Latte

Coffee made with espresso and
steamed milk, topped with a small amount of foam.

Cold Brew

Coffee steeped in cold water for an extended period,
resulting in a smooth, less acidic flavor.
