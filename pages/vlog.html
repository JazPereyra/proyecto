Raspberry Cheesecake

  • Mix crushed graham crackers with melted butter and
    bake for 10 minutes.
  • Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
    Add eggs one at a time.
  • Gently fold in fresh or thawed raspberries into
    the cream cheese mixture.
  • Pour filling over the crust, smooth the top,
    and bake at 325°F for 50 minutes
  • Allow the cheesecake to cool completely
  • Top with additional raspberries and a dusting
    of powdered sugar before slicing and serving.

Chocolate Lava Cake

  • Set your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Melt 1/2 cup of butter with 1 cup of chocolate chips.
  • Add 2 eggs and 2 egg yolks, mixing well.
    Fold in 1/2 cup of flour.
  • Butter and flour four ramekins or muffin cups
    to prevent sticking.
  • Pour the batter into the prepared ramekins.
  • Bake for 12-14 minutes until the edges are set but the
    centers are still soft.
  • Let the cakes cool for a minute, then carefully
    invert onto plates.
